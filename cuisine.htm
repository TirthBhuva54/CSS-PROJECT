North Indian Cuisine

North Indian is known for its rich,aromatic,and flavoful dishes often cooked with ghee and speices:

  • Biryani:A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices and meat or vegetables.
  • Butter Chicken:A creamy,tomato-based curry that's favourite worldwide.
  • Panner tikka:Marinated panner cubes grilled to perfection.

South Indian Cuisine

South India offers a wide array of dishes with a perfect blend of spices and tangy flavors:

  • Masala Dosa:A crispy rice creps stuffed with spicy potato filling.
  • Idli and SambharSoft rice cakes served with a tangy lentil-based vegetable stew.
  • Chettinad ChickenA fiery,flavorful Cchicken dish from Tamil Nadu.
